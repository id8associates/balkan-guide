Discover Albania

From the pristine Ionian coastline and Ottoman-era stone towns to the rugged Accursed Mountains and ancient Illyrian ruins — Albania is Europe's last hidden gem, waiting to be explored.

Getting There

✈️ Main Airports

  • Tirana Airport (TIA) - The main gateway, 17km from capital
  • Corfu Airport (CFU) - Nearby in Greece, ferry to Saranda
  • Podgorica Airport (TGD) - Nearby in Montenegro, access to northern Albania
